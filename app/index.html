About Me

Having recently completed an immersive front-end class at The Iron Yard in Atlanta, I am now a freelance web developer working primarily with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. The experience of developing a project from the faintest glimmer of an idea to something on a page that serves a function is a tremendous thrill! Please take a look at some of my projects. I enjoy working on individual 'pet' projects such as the family picture album. Even more, I enjoy the team environment where I can collaborate with others and contribute by solving problems and moving ideas toward something tangible.



Movie Sidekick

Movie Sidekick was an idea of mine that essentially was to take the "Goodreads" concept to the world of movies.

The app allows the user to search movies, review movies, connect with other users, and even watch movies on amazon or purchase tickets to current films.

We used a number of Angular tools, services, controllers and directives on this fairly complex app.


Code: https://github.com/MovieSidekick/SidekickApp
